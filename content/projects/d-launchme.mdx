# LaunchMe: From Resume to Live Website in 60 Seconds

[LaunchMe](https://launchme.site) is a no-code platform that transforms resumes and LinkedIn profiles into beautiful, customizable portfolio websites — instantly. Upload a PDF or paste a LinkedIn URL, pick a theme, and your site is live.

## The Problem

Every professional needs an online presence, but building a portfolio site takes time, technical skill, or money. Most people default to a bare LinkedIn profile or skip it entirely. LaunchMe removes every barrier — no coding, no design skills, no hosting setup.

## How It Works

1. **Upload Your Resume** — Drop a PDF or paste your LinkedIn profile URL.
2. **AI Parses Everything** — Work history, education, skills, and contact info are extracted automatically using AI.
3. **Pick a Theme & Publish** — Choose from 8 premium themes, customize your URL slug, and go live at `launchme.site/yourname`.

## Key Features

### 8 Premium Themes
Choose from Classic, Terminal, Warm, Brutalist, Gradient, Neon, Sky Blue, and Lavender — each with a distinct visual identity suited to different professional styles.

### AI Resume Parsing
Powered by Groq's LLM, FitMyCV intelligently extracts and structures your resume data using the JSON Resume schema — no manual data entry needed.

### Custom URLs
Every site gets a clean, shareable URL: `launchme.site/yourname`. Make it easy for recruiters and clients to find you.

### Built-in Contact Form
Visitors can reach you directly through your portfolio. Messages are stored and delivered to your inbox.

### Interactive Globe
A live Mapbox-powered globe visualization shows all published LaunchMe sites around the world — a growing community of professionals.

### Edit Anytime
Update your work history, skills, education, and theme whenever you want through the built-in editor.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: Next.js 16, React 19, Tailwind CSS v4, Framer Motion
- **AI**: Groq SDK (llama-3.1-8b-instant) for resume parsing
- **Database**: MongoDB with Mongoose
- **Auth**: NextAuth.js with Google and GitHub OAuth
- **Maps**: Mapbox GL for the interactive globe

## Traction

800+ portfolio websites generated in the first 5 days of launch, with an active community sharing their sites on LinkedIn.

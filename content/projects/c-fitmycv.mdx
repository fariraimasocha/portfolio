# FitMyCV: Tailor Your CV to Any Job in 47 Seconds

[FitMyCV](https://fitmycv.co) is an AI-powered platform that transforms generic resumes into job-specific, ATS-optimized CVs and cover letters. Paste a job listing URL, and FitMyCV rewrites your CV with the right keywords, structure, and language to pass automated screening systems and impress recruiters.

## The Problem

Most job seekers send the same CV to every employer. But 75% of applications are rejected by ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems) before a human ever reads them. Manually tailoring each application takes an average of 3.5 hours — and after dozens of rejections, burnout sets in fast.

## How It Works

1. **Paste a Job URL** — Drop a link from LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, or any job board.
2. **AI Tailors Your CV** — FitMyCV extracts the job requirements and rewrites your CV with matching keywords woven in naturally.
3. **Download & Apply** — Get your tailored CV and a custom cover letter as polished PDFs, ready to submit.

## Key Features

### ATS Keyword Optimization
The AI extracts requirements from job postings and weaves them naturally into your CV, dramatically improving your chances of passing automated screening.

### AI Cover Letter Generation
Every tailored CV comes with a personalized, role-specific cover letter generated in seconds — no more staring at a blank page.

### Match Score Analysis
See exactly how well your CV matches each job with an ATS compatibility score (0–100%) and actionable suggestions to close the gap.

### Company Research & Interview Prep
When you paste a job link, FitMyCV automatically generates a company research brief with insights on the company, role expectations, and interview prep tips.

### Professional PDF Templates
Choose from six professionally designed templates — Minimal, Executive, Creative, Tech, Classic, and Bold — each optimized for different industries.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: Next.js 16, React 19, Tailwind CSS v4
- **AI**: OpenAI GPT-4o-mini for CV tailoring and cover letter generation, Groq for job parsing
- **Database**: MongoDB with Mongoose
- **Auth**: NextAuth.js with Google OAuth
- **PDF**: jsPDF for generation, unpdf for parsing uploaded resumes

## Results

FitMyCV takes the average application time from 3.5 hours down to under a minute. Users consistently report higher callback rates after switching from generic CVs to AI-tailored applications.
